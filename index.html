Support

We're here to help.

Connect Strava

During onboarding, Inflexion will send you a secure link to authorize your Strava account. Tap it, log in, and authorize read access to your activities. That's it.

If you need to reconnect, use the /connect_strava command in the bot.

Data & Privacy

Inflexion only reads your Strava activities. We never post on your behalf, never share your data, and never sell it. You can revoke access from your Strava settings at any time.
